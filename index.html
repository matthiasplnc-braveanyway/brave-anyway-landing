The fine print, in plain words.

Is this written by AI?

The personalization is, the foundations aren't. Every situation follows a narrative framework built and reviewed with child psychiatrists. The technology fills in your child's world inside those rails; it never improvises on the emotional content.

What about my child's data?

We ask only for a first name, an age and a few favorite things. No photos, no last names, no location. This information is used solely to create your stories and is never sold or shared.

What ages is it for?

Ages 2 to 10. Every story adapts its length, vocabulary and emotional depth to your child's age. A story about the first day of school reads very differently at 3 than at 7.

How much does it cost?

Chapter one of any story is always free to read, no card needed. After that, try Brave Anyway free for 7 days, then it's $12.99/month for unlimited stories and unlimited kids. Cancel anytime.
